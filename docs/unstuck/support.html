Unstuck

Unstuck removes the decision: dump every task you've been avoiding into one list, tap "Pick one for me," and the app locks you into a single task with a calm, full-screen 2-minute countdown. One task. Two minutes. No deciding. Built for ADHD brains and plain decision fatigue.

Available on the App Store for iPhone.
