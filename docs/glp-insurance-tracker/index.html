Privacy Policy

Ozempic Insurance Tracker  ·  Effective date: May 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Ozempic Insurance Tracker mobile application ("App", "we", "us") handles your information. Please read it carefully.

Information We Collect

All data you enter into the App — medication cost entries, insurance preferences, and generated appeal letters — is stored locally on your device only using your device's secure local storage. We do not operate servers that receive or store this data.

Automatically Collected Information

The App collects limited anonymous technical data to ensure stability and manage purchases:

  • Crash reports to diagnose technical issues via Sentry.
  • Purchase & subscription status to manage in-app purchases via RevenueCat.

None of this data is linked to your personal identity. It does not include the health or insurance information you enter into the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Sentry – crash reporting
  • RevenueCat – in-app purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Your data lives on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App. Anonymous analytics and crash data retained by third-party services follow their respective retention policies.

Your Rights

Because your personal health and insurance data is stored solely on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it. For requests related to analytics or crash data, please contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
